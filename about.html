Our Story

Comrade Furniture was established in 2010 with a simple mission: to create beautiful, functional, and durable furniture that enhances the lives of our customers. Located in the heart of Makerere Kikoni, Kampala, we have grown from a small workshop to one of Uganda's premier furniture retailers.

Our founder, John Mukasa, started with a passion for woodworking and a vision to combine traditional craftsmanship with modern design. Today, that vision continues to guide our work as we create furniture that stands the test of time.

At Comrade Furniture, we believe that quality furniture should be accessible to everyone. That's why we work directly with local artisans and source sustainable materials to create pieces that are both beautiful and affordable.
